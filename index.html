Doren Daigle is a GIA Graduate Gemologist and master jeweler based in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Trained at the Gemological Institute of America — the most respected gemological authority in the world — Doren brings a level of precision and knowledge to his craft that is rarely found outside the world's great jewelry houses.

Every piece begins with a conversation. Doren works closely with each client to understand not just what they want, but why they want it — the occasion, the person, the feeling they hope to carry for a lifetime. The result is always singular. Never replicated.

Rooted in Louisiana and built on the belief that fine jewelry should be personal above all else, Daigle Jewelry exists for those who refuse to settle for anything ordinary.
